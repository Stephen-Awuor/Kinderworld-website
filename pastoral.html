Knowledge and Values.

Together with this, we also aim to build strong Islamic foundations, which include development of positive Islamic thoughts, good manners, respect and a deep sense of love for Islam as a way of life.

Conducive Environment

Kinderworld aims at providing a conducive learning environment in all its institutions that nurture sound Islamic and Secular Education in order to develop the spiritual, moral, intellectual and physical character of a child in accordance with the Laws of Allah Ta’ala as shown by example of Prophet Muhammad (SAW)

Future Religious leaders

To inculcate in learners the consciousness of Allah Taala and of the Qur’an as a source of intellectual, emotional, spiritual and physical growth as shown by the Holy Prophet Muhammad (SAW).

The strive for excellence in Islamic character.

We enhance our students’ spirituality through wonders of the natural phenomena, congregational prayers, Qur’an recitation and memorization, Qur’an studies and observe Islamic Manners.

Message from the Head Of Pastoral Studies

“Education is a shared commitment between dedicated teachers, motivated students and enthusiastic parents with high expectation The parent-teacher trust and mutual understanding is a real secret to a child’s optimum development. A balance of education at home and school moulds a student’s actual learning. A good parent teacher relations leads the child to be positive towards attending school. Teachers and parents provide a vital support system to help students flourish. Parents are a child’s first role model; they have a key role in shaping up their character. Children tend to imitate their parents and try to behave in a much of similar manner". To our Directors, Parents, Teachers and Support staff I say thank you for making Kinderworld International School a family of Hope for Our Children.

Ust. Innocent Anis
